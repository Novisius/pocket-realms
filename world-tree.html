World Tree

A world tree planted by Fomori within the blind eternities, between the Edge and the Multiverse, each seeded with life and since left to their own devices.

Insectile sapients and Humans had been taken from the Exoverse, while other insectile sapients and Humans had been taken from the Multiverse. They were each placed onto the fruits, the many realms of the world tree, along with simple plant life, a curation vault to ensure a smooth transition, and, of all things, Slivers.

Each realm is aligned with a single color identity, the branches and roots of the cosmic tree, encasing them, draining them of certain colors of mana, and injecting them with other colors of mana.

Growth of the Tree

The Slivers were not brought in without purpose, they are integeral to the lifecycle of this tree itself, growing to a certain point, nebulously greater than the prior point, where then the hive slaughters their members, gives all of the Manastones they have amassed, and even temporarily kill their territory, causing a wave of incredible and arguably near infinite quantities of mana into the tree. This causes the tree to gain a growth spurt, the expansion of the realms, the expansion of the tree.

These events causes a sound of the air itself tearing apart, earthquakes to rock the entirety of each realm. The lands multiply, the atmosphere fills in empty space as it is refilled. Despite the cataclysmic process of realmatic expansion, deaths caused by them are rare, injuries not so much, temporary hearing loss not being particularly uncommon.

These events, due to the sound and the sudden lack of Skep activity, are called Crashes. As of now, there have been eighteen such events, causing the differences in realm sizes between strata to become readily apparent.

Fruit of the World Tree

The World Tree holds thirty two realms, one for each color identity. These realms are separated by strata within the canopies of the world tree.

Roots of the Tree

The roots of this great cosmic tree are grown around a single realm, these roots sap out the colors of mana, forcing the realm to be colorless.

  • Komas - A realm devoid of colors of mana, unique for its mechanical Slivers.
