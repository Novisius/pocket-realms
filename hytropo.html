Hytropo

The realm is named Hytrop(i/a/o). (Hi-trope-(ee/ah/oh))

Each denizen calls it either Hytropi, Hytropa, or Hytropo; based upon the gender they identify with. -i for non-binary or fluid, -a for feminine, and -o for masculine.

-e (ey) is another suffix, used for neutral gender.
-u (oo) is another suffix used for unknown genders or to be formal.
Attaching a -y to a suffix turns it plural.

Hytrope and Hytropu are uncommon forms of the realm’s name, but possible to encounter.

Within the language of the denizens of the realm, all nouns used these suffixes. To use a car as an example, if no one was driving it, the word would end in -e, as it is a simple object without a will or soul, but if a man was driving it, it would end in -o and a woman driving it, -a.

Names utilize this method as well, however, it is based on the person who owns the name to choose a suffix, unlike with objects.

Terrain

The center of the realm is a series of evershifting cracks surrounding the liquid metal realmic core. Occasional breaches to the metal allow slivers to escape or scant growths of Moxite/Etherium/Crystals (MEC) to grow out.

These cracks widen out into wide caverns, each one relatively diverse. Some have their own flora and fauna, others have lakes and streams, a few even have enough consistent MEC to serve as light sources. Most of the realm's moderates have found homes and refuge within these caverns, in particular, the ones with consistent light.

These caverns are not simply in the ground, they are within the horizon dominating pillar of black stone that spans many kilometers across the center of the realm. Tunnels weave through this structure in a mess that few even dare to consider to map out, leaving only a few well known, established, and predictably safe pathways to and from the caverns and the outside.

Outside of this spire lies a realm of darkness. The clouds black as they choke out the sun, the air near poison to those unacclimated, a caustic and toxic fog clings to the barren ground like brine in the ocean. Above it flies the sanctuaries, both arcane and technological, circling the spire in a slow dance. The Edgebound and Versebound humans of this realm had decended into a rather violent form of tribalism, centuries ago, which resulted in a catastrophic war. What remains of the realm is the aftermath of that conflict, grand magics against devastating technologies. Believe it or not, the environment has recovered by leaps and bounds since then. Before they could destroy everything, they came to an agreement, they would not see each other ever again. The Edgebound humans were relugated to one side of the spire, the Versebound humans to the other. They both crafted sanctuaries above the devastation, but at some point, they moved. Best theories maintain that it was due to a crash that caused a sanctuary to shift into line of sight of the other side, which prompted them to shift, which prompted their neighbors to shift, which prompted the other side to shift. So now, they circle the spire slowly, perhaps one kilometer an hour if that. During the war, the moderates had more or less taken the innards of the spire, and that area is officially considered to be 'neutral ground'. Which why it is now where most realmic trade occurs.

Further still from this area, the lands have begun to heal, in a generous sense. Waters are contaminated with oils, chemicals, and poisons. The air would still be hard to breath for you or I, leading to a significantly shorter and arguably painful, life. The ground is rocky and irradiated, not enough to kill now, but enough to induce cancers in the next decade or so. What little plantlife has been able to claw their way back to a notable presence is venomous and poisonous, not actually on purpose, but as a byproduct of the air, water, and soil. The plants themselves are usually grasses or lichen of some variety, depending on the location.
